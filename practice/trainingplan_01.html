🏀 U14 Boys Practice Plan – 1 Hour

This structured 60-minute session is built around key teaching points. Use the links in the 🎥 Teaching Points by Concept page to review each concept with example video clips.

Time Drill / Activity Focus Area Teaching Point Concept Notes
0–5 min Dynamic Warm-Up Mobility & Injury Prevention Warm-Up Focus on hip, knee and ankle mobility
5–15 min 1v1 Closeout & Contain Drill On-ball Defense Closeouts Teach proper stance, angle & recovery
15–25 min Shorts into Pistols Breakdown Offensive Actions Shorts → Pistols Read defense, pass or keep decisions
25–35 min 3-on-3 Live (with scoring system) Spacing, Decision-Making Motion Offense Use scoring rules: +1 for extra pass, -1 for turnovers
35–45 min Shell Drill (4v4) Help & Recover Shell Defense Call out positions: “Help”, “Ball”, “Gap”
45–55 min Transition – 3 Lane Fast Break Offense in Transition Transition Offense Run wide, fill lanes, make early decisions
55–60 min Free Throws + Team Review Focus + Pressure Free Throws Pressure FT shooting competition + recap 1 key takeaway
